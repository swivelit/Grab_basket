### Q1: How long does it take to generate?
**A**: Usually 2-10 seconds. If you have 500+ products with images, it may take up to 30 seconds.

### Q2: Can I export only specific categories?
**A**: Not yet! Currently exports all products. Use Excel export + filtering as workaround.

### Q3: What if I don't have product images?
**A**: No problem! Products without images show a "ğŸ“¦ No Image Available" placeholder. Your catalog will still be created.

**A**: Not yet! The design is fixed for now. Future updates may include customization options.

### Q5: How big is the PDF file?
**A**: Depends on product count:

**A**: Yes! You can export from mobile browser. PDF downloads to your phone.

**A**: Yes! Only you (the seller) can export your catalog. Customers see only what you share with them.
